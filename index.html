About NIRMAN

Where innovation meets intelligence

What is NIRMAN?

NIRMAN is a premier offline hackathon that brings together passionate students to explore the intersection of artificial intelligence and creative problem-solving. It's a platform where ideas transform into reality through collaboration, coding, and innovation.

AI & Creation Focus

This hackathon emphasizes leveraging AI technologies to build meaningful solutions. From machine learning to intelligent automation, participants will explore cutting-edge tools while maintaining a human-centered approach to creation and design.

Team-Based Excellence

NIRMAN is designed for teams of 2-4 students who work together through every challenge. It's an offline, college-level competition that fosters collaboration, encourages peer learning, and celebrates collective achievement in a professional environment.

Hackathon Format

Four levels of innovation and excellence

1

Gaming Round

Begin your journey with non-coding, offline AI-based games designed to test strategic thinking, pattern recognition, and team coordination. This round sets the foundation and gets everyone thinking algorithmically without diving into code yet.

2

Idea & Problem Solving

Receive a domain-specific challenge with defined constraints. Teams brainstorm, research, and develop a comprehensive solution strategy. This round evaluates your ability to understand requirements, think critically, and plan effectively.

3

NIRMAN Creation Round

The coding begins. Transform your ideas into working prototypes using AI frameworks, APIs, and development tools. This is where creation happens‚Äîbuilding, iterating, debugging, and refining your solution within a time-bound environment.

4

Final AI Pitch War

Present your creation to a panel of expert judges. Demonstrate your prototype, explain your approach, defend your decisions, and answer questions. This final round tests your communication, confidence, and ability to articulate technical concepts.

Eligibility & Rules

Guidelines for participation

Open for College Students

All currently enrolled college and university students are eligible to participate. Bring your passion for technology and innovation.

Team Size: 2‚Äì4 Members

Form teams of 2 to 4 students. Solo participation is not allowed. Collaboration is at the heart of NIRMAN.

Offline Participation

This is an in-person event. All team members must be physically present throughout the hackathon duration.

Original Work Only

All submissions must be original work created during the hackathon. Pre-built solutions or plagiarized code will result in disqualification.

Judges' Decision Final

The decisions made by the judging panel are final and binding. All participants must respect the evaluation process.
